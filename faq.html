General

NiPa Player is a premium IPTV media player for iOS, Android, Android TV, and macOS. It lets you stream Live TV, Movies, and Series from your own IPTV provider. NiPa Player does not provide, host, or stream any content — you bring your own playlists.
4 platforms: iPhone & iPad (iOS 15+), Android phones & tablets (Android 7+), Android TV / Google TV, and macOS (Apple Silicon & Intel).
NiPa Player offers a 21-day free trial with full access to all features. After the trial, a subscription (monthly, yearly, or lifetime) is required.
The app is available in 28 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, Arabic, Bengali, Turkish, Polish, Dutch, Indonesian, Malay, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Finnish, Vietnamese, Thai, Urdu, Hungarian, Serbian, and Albanian.

EPG (TV Guide)

The Electronic Program Guide (EPG) shows a visual timeline of what's currently playing, what's coming up, and what has aired on your live TV channels — similar to a traditional TV guide grid.
From the Live TV section, tap the "TV Guide" tab at the top of the screen.
Yes! Tap any upcoming program in the TV Guide and select "Remind Me". You'll receive a push notification when the program is about to start. Reminders work even when the app is closed.
The EPG data depends on your IPTV provider. If your provider doesn't include EPG data, the TV Guide will show "No program guide available." This is not a NiPa Player issue — contact your provider.

Chromecast

Yes! You can cast live streams and VOD content to any Chromecast-compatible device on your network.

2. Start playing any stream.
3. Tap the Cast icon (📺) in the player controls.

Cloud Sync

Cloud Sync securely synchronizes your playlists and favorites across all your devices using your Google or Apple account. Your data is encrypted with AES-256 using a key derived from your account.
1. Go to Settings > Account & Sync.
2. Sign in with Google or Apple.
3. Tap "Sync Now" to perform your first sync.
Playlist configurations (names, credentials, URLs) and favorites (across all playlists).
Yes. All sensitive playlist data is encrypted with AES-256 before being stored in the cloud. The encryption key is derived from your account and never leaves your device.
Sync is triggered manually by tapping "Sync Now". There is a 60-second cooldown between syncs to prevent excessive requests.

Troubleshooting

1. Check your internet connection.
2. Verify your playlist credentials — go to Settings, edit your playlist, and re-enter your credentials.
3. Update your playlist — go to Settings and tap "Update Playlist" to re-download channel data.
4. Check your IPTV provider — the issue may be on their end.
Check your internet speed — IPTV streaming requires a stable connection (10+ Mbps recommended). Try switching to a wired connection if on Android TV. Some channels may have higher bitrates that require faster connections.
After updating a playlist, go back to the main menu and navigate to Live TV, Movies, or Series. Your channels should be there organized by categories. Try using Search to find specific content.
Go to Settings > Help & Feedback to send us an email directly from the app. You can also reach us at dev@nipaplayer.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.
